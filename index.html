Once the B2B marketplace is established (Phase 1), Canvas Protocol will introduce direct crypto rewards (e.g., ETH/Stablecoins) to end-users for successfully completing interactive CAPTCHAs. This phase aims to drive broader adoption of the Ethereum L2 and EigenLayer ecosystem and establish a new standard for user verification online.
